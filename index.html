About Our Services

Compassionate Care Where You Need It Most

At Professional Home Care Services, we are committed to delivering exceptional healthcare solutions right at your doorstep. Our team of skilled doctors, nurses, and medical staff ensures professional, compassionate, and timely care tailored to your specific needs.

We understand that hospital visits can be stressful, especially for elderly patients, post-operative cases, and those with chronic conditions. That's why we bring comprehensive medical services to your home, maintaining the highest standards of care while providing comfort and convenience.

Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone in Rawalpindi and Islamabad, especially those who find it difficult to visit hospitals or clinics regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently provide our home healthcare services throughout Rawalpindi and Islamabad. Our team can reach you anywhere within these cities.

How quickly can a healthcare provider arrive?

For urgent cases, we can typically arrange for a healthcare professional to visit your home within 1-2 hours. For scheduled visits, we'll coordinate a time that works best for you.

Are your medical professionals qualified?

Absolutely. All our healthcare providers are fully qualified, licensed, and experienced in their respective fields. We carefully vet each professional before they join our team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is collected after the service is provided unless prior arrangements have been made.

Can you provide long-term care services?

Yes, we offer both short-term and long-term care solutions. We can develop customized care plans for chronic conditions, elderly care, or post-operative recovery that extend for weeks or months as needed.
